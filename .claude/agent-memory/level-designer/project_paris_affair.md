1965 spy thriller stealth game ("The Paris Affair"). Eve is the player character (BQA agent). Three sections currently spec'd with civilians: Plaza (strollers), Restaurant (diners, 6-8 cap), Eiffel viewing platforms (tourists). Five pillars: 1=Comedy Without Punchlines, 2=Discovery Rewards Patience, 3=Stealth is Theatre Not Punishment, 4=Iconic Locations, 5=Period Authenticity Over Modernization.

**Why:** Informs all level authoring decisions — especially civilian placement, anchor placement, visual variety guidance, and "zero UI" constraints.

**How to apply:** When reviewing or authoring any level document, cross-check civilian placement against Pillar 5 (no HUD markers, no floating nameplates) and Pillar 2 (BQA contacts hidden in plain sight). Restaurant is the worst-case performance section (8-civilian cap, narrow corridors). Civilian AI GDD is system #15, authored 2026-04-25.
